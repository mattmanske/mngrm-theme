About

  • MNGRMS are impossible to make by hand. Each is made from about 350 solid metal cylinders.

  • Each individual 1/2 inch MNGRM is made up of over 2 feet of solid metal piping.

  • Each custom MNGRM will arrive 3 - 4 weeks from the date of order

  • Our chains are made from the finest matching material and not plated so they will last with age.

  • The 1/2 inch pendant necklace chains include an adjustable clasp, allowing the chain to be lengthened from 16 - 20 inches.

  • We built the MNGRM structures off of the contemporary sans- serif Arial typeface.
