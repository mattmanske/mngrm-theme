About

  • MNGRMS are impossible to make by hand. We use the latest in 3D printing technology to make our complex precious metal pendants.

  • Each individual 1/2 inch MNGRM is made up of over 2 feet of solid metal piping.

  • Each custom MNGRM will arrive 3 - 4 weeks from the date of order

  • Our chains are made from the finest matching material and will last with age. Necklaces include a long 30 inch chain. Keychains include a 1.5 inch keyring chain.

  • MNGRM is designed from our favorite contemporary sans- seriff Arial typeface.
